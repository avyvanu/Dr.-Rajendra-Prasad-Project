About Us

 

The Siddhartha Sanskritik Samiti (Regd) is a social and cultural organization. It is fruitful efforts of well organized and visionary people of Bihar settled in Bangalore, who shaped the Samiti during 1970s with the objective of togetherness, unity, creativity and to develop cultural activities among the people of Bangalore irrespective of Caste, creed and religion.

 

The Samiti has been devoted to extend coordination, cooperation among the people of Karnataka in general and Bangalore in particular and to exchange the cultural values of each other to promote the national integrity. To turn this green into reality the Samiti has established a Trust as “Rajendra Babu Memorial Trust”.

 

About Rajendra Babu Memorial Trust

 

The Rajendra Babu Memorial Trust was established in the year 1986 in the name of First President of our country, Deshratna Dr. Rajendra Prasad.

 

We at the trust follow the principle of true Gandhian, Dr. Rajendra Babu “Simple Living – High Thinking” in our day today activities.

Ø    The Board of Trustees consists of eminent personalities from the field of Education, Business, Industry, Social work and other Sectors of the Society.

Ø    We have imported the Thought of maxim of Dr. Rajendra Prasad from the enriched cultural State of Bihar, which has produced many brilliant and eminent personalities and countributed to the growth of nation and spread the light of knowledge in form of Buddha, Mahavir, etc. Since inception of our institution in 1986, we have been contributing for the social upliftment and welfare of the migrated people of Bihar in particular and in general the mass of Karnataka. We have organized many cultural activities to preponderate and assimilate all class of people by celebrating many festival to enlarge the moral and cosmic value of the mankind.

Ø    We have always been the front runners with our maximum capacity of contribution towards the national disaster and natural calamity. The aim of our institution is to promote the Education in the society and help the poor to acquire knowledge by establishing school and college by infusing the discipline, high moral and high thought as principled and enumerated by Late. Dr. Rajendra Prasad.

 

Our Main Objectives

(A)             To setup an Educational Institute for Basic, Professional and Technical Courses.

a.     To promote education for all, irrespective of cast, creed and color, rich or poor.

b.     To impart the great social and moral values and inculcate rich ancient Indian Culture.

c.      To impart functional literacy to make self-dependant with concept of “earling while learning”.

d.     To start short and long term residential courses to refresh and update with latest development in the field of education.

e.      To start adult education through distance learning and generate employment to remove poverty.

(B)             To setup a Research Institute for the development of intelligence and education in children.

a.     To find and create interest and quest in children while playing and in other while working

b.     To develop positive attitude and enable to achieve all round success in life as well as in career.

c.      To provide education and make all efforts to enable the disabled children to be independent.

 

 

 

Future Plan

 

1.     To establish school, college for the poor, deserving students and downtrodden people of society.

2.     To promote the cultural values in the society among the children and youth.

3.     To calibrate the social values and provide self-confidence to deal and tackle with situation in life.

4.     to organize all such function and cultural activities to promote national integration.

5.     To establish and maintain home and institution for poor, Disabled, Physically and Mentally Handicapped persons.

6.     To grant Scholarships, Medals, Prizes, Books, Stipends, Awards, to deserving students, Scholars and Research works of all cast and creeds.

 

OUR VISION

 

Ø    To establish an” Educational Institute for Intelligence Development and Research” of international standard with ancient Indian Heritage and cultural values in the name of First President of India Dr. Rajendra Prasad. An unique Institute for building career and shaping of a holistic personalities. Building a temple of learning and growing with strong Rich Cultural Values.

Ø    Education with a difference “Bringing awareness and creating opportunity to generate self employment by providing functional literacy to all, particularly to rural and underprivileged people of society through distance learning as well as classroom study”

 

To provide total innovative education as per the interest and intelligence of the person, developing the skills and attitude with adequate knowledge and making the person self-dependent in the society.

 

Our Strength

 

Cooperation from more than one lakh members of the Samiti in Bangalore, Karnataka and coordination from various associations functioning in Bangalore for the similar objectives.
