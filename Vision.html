OUR VISION

 

Ø    To establish an” Educational Institute for Intelligence Development and Research” of international standard with ancient Indian Heritage and cultural values in the name of First President of India Dr. Rajendra Prasad. An unique Institute for building career and shaping of a holistic personalities. Building a temple of learning and growing with strong Rich Cultural Values.

Ø    Education with a difference “Bringing awareness and creating opportunity to generate self employment by providing functional literacy to all, particularly to rural and underprivileged people of society through distance learning as well as classroom study”

 

To provide total innovative education as per the interest and intelligence of the person, developing the skills and attitude with adequate knowledge and making the person self-dependent in the society.
